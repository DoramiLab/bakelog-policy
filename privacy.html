Privacy Policy

BakeLog  ·  Effective date: April 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you choose to link an account, we collect your email address (email sign-in) or a unique identifier from Google or Apple (social sign-in). Anonymous use is fully supported without providing any personal information.

User Content

Recipes, bake logs, and notes you create are stored in Firebase Firestore. Pro users who enable photo sync have their photos uploaded to Firebase Storage.

Camera & Photos

Camera access is used to capture photos of recipe books or bake logs. Photos may be sent to Google Gemini AI for recipe extraction and are not retained on our servers after processing. Pro users may optionally sync bake log photos to Firebase Storage.

Usage Data

We collect anonymized analytics data (app launches, feature usage, session duration) via Firebase Analytics to improve the app. This data is linked to an anonymous device identifier, not your personal identity.

Crash Reports

We collect crash and error reports via Firebase Crashlytics to diagnose and fix bugs.

Purchase History

We track in-app purchase events (e.g. Pro upgrade, AI credit purchases) for analytics purposes. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and sync app functionality across devices (account-linked users)
  • Improve app quality through analytics and crash reporting
  • Process AI recipe extraction via Gemini

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, storage, analytics, crash reporting
    firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Gemini AI — Recipe extraction from images
  • Apple In-App Purchase — Payment processing

4. Data Retention

Anonymous accounts and their data are retained until you delete the app. Linked accounts retain data until account deletion is requested. Crash reports are retained for 90 days.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. You can revoke camera or photo library access at any time in your device settings.
